# prompts/total_slide_prompt.md


Write ONE executive summary for the TOTAL industry slide (20-25 words).


## TONE


Analytically honest with constructive framing where warranted. Match the balanced tone from last quarter's approved samples.


TOTAL metrics:
{total_fact_block_or_lines}


## HOW TO LEAD (choose the clearest signal)


Lead with the metric that best represents the industry story this quarter (dollars/revenue OR units/demand). Mention the main driver briefly (pricing/ASP vs demand).


## BALANCED APPROACH


- If accuracy is tight: lead with the achievement, explain what drove it
- If variance is larger: acknowledge honestly, explain the market drivers
- Keep it executive and insight-led (not a list of figures)


## EXAMPLES FROM LAST QUARTER


- "Forecasts tracked closely within 2.2 percentage points of dollars as the industry contracted slightly versus expectations, with pricing gains offsetting softer unit demand."
- "Industry dollars aligned with forecasts as stronger-than-anticipated ASP inflation offset softer unit sales."
- "The industry outperformed forecasts, driven by stronger-than-expected unit performance that compensated for marginally softer pricing. Dollars came in within 2.8 percentage points of estimates."
- "While unit performance tracked reasonably close to forecast, ASP growth underdelivered, resulting in a dollar shortfall versus projections."


## RULES


- 20-25 words maximum
- Spell out "percentage points" (never abbreviate)
- Include 1 percentage point figure (max 2)
- Explain the key driver briefly (demand vs pricing)
- NO superlatives: "perfect", "exceptional", "outstanding"
- NO forward-looking speculation


Return only the insight text.
